About Us

At Aqua Elektron, we're more than just accommodation; we're your gateway to a vibrant university experience. With our four exceptional residences – Park Village, Amberfield Village, Taung Village, and Moahi Village – we've redefined student living at Vaal University of Technology. Our commitment to comfort, community, and convenience ensures that every student can thrive in an environment that feels like home. Discover a world of academic support, social connections, and a place to create unforgettable memories. Choose Aqua Elektron, where student life meets extraordinary living.

Our Services

WI-FI

Stay connected with lightning-fast, unlimited WiFi – your gateway to seamless online learning, streaming, and staying in touch with loved ones. At Aqua Elektron, we make sure buffering is a thing of the past, so you can focus on what matters most.

Transportation

Enjoy stress-free commutes with our complimentary transport services! Hop on our busses and sprinters, offering free rides to and from campus, so you can focus on what matters most – your education.

Security

Your safety is our top priority. Our comprehensive security services, both at the gate and inside our premises, provide round-the-clock protection, ensuring you enjoy peace of mind throughout your stay.

Backup Water Supply

Stay worry-free with our Backup Water Supply Service. When municipal water interruptions strike, Aqua Elektron ensures you never run dry, guaranteeing a continuous flow of water to keep your daily life uninterrupted.

Laundry Room

Unleash the convenience of 24/7 laundry service at Aqua Elektron. Our fully equipped laundry room, featuring top-of-the-line washing and drying machines, puts student convenience first. Clean, fresh, and always accessible – we're here to make your life easier!.

Cafeteria

Indulge in a world of flavor at our student-friendly cafeteria. From delicious, affordable meals to a cozy hangout spot, we're here to fuel your study sessions and satisfy your taste buds. Explore a diverse menu, grab a bite, and enjoy the perfect blend of taste and convenience.
